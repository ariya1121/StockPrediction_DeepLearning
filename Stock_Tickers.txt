1. Major Stock Exchanges and Their Stocks
NYSE (New York Stock Exchange)
Examples:

AMZN: Amazon
MSFT: Microsoft
AAPL: Apple
TSLA: Tesla
GOOG or GOOGL: Alphabet (Google)
NASDAQ
Examples:

INTC: Intel
NFLX: Netflix
NVDA: Nvidia
META: Meta Platforms (Facebook)
Other Global Stock Exchanges

LSE (London Stock Exchange): BP.L, HSBA.L
BSE/NSE (India): RELIANCE.BO, TCS.NS, INFY.BO
TSE (Tokyo Stock Exchange): 7203.T (Toyota)
ASX (Australian Stock Exchange): BHP.AX, CBA.AX
2. Indices
You can retrieve data for popular indices like:

^GSPC: S&P 500
^DJI: Dow Jones Industrial Average
^IXIC: NASDAQ Composite
^NSEI: Nifty 50 (India)
^FTSE: FTSE 100 (UK)
^N225: Nikkei 225 (Japan)
3. ETFs (Exchange-Traded Funds)
Popular ETFs available on Yahoo Finance:

SPY: SPDR S&P 500 ETF Trust
QQQ: Invesco QQQ Trust
IVV: iShares Core S&P 500 ETF
VTI: Vanguard Total Stock Market ETF
4. Commodities
CL=F: Crude Oil
GC=F: Gold Futures
SI=F: Silver Futures
NG=F: Natural Gas
5. Currencies
EURUSD=X: Euro to US Dollar
GBPUSD=X: British Pound to US Dollar
JPYUSD=X: Japanese Yen to US Dollar
BTC-USD: Bitcoin to US Dollar

BTC-USD: Bitcoin
